About Situation Monitor

Situation Monitor aggregates news from RSS feeds, social data from X/Twitter via Grok sweeps, and market quotes into a single dashboard. It runs a knowledge graph pipeline that extracts entities, clusters related items, and tracks evolving situations.

Features

  • Real-time RSS feed aggregation from global news sources
  • X/social data collection via Grok sweeps through OpenRouter
  • Market quotes tracking
  • AI-powered daily briefings
  • Entity extraction and situation tracking via knowledge graph
  • On-demand article reading with translation support
